User Suggestion

We're excited to continue perfecting and augmenting the website. As such, your feedback and suggestions are invaluable! In order to help your suggestion be as helpful as possible, please take a second to read our do's and don'ts.

Do's

  • Do describe the suggestion in as much detail as possible. If it references a particular page or type of page, include an example link.
  • Do leave your contact information if you wish to be contacted with a follow-up to your suggestion.

Don'ts

  • Don't spam the suggestions page with multiple entries for the same request.
  • Don't be concerned if it takes some time to respond to or implement your suggestion. Developing changes can be a time-consuming process.
