About

FAMAT

Mu Alpha Theta describes their purpose on their website,

Mu Alpha Theta is the National High School and Two-Year College Mathematics Honor Society with over 113,500 student members in June 2016 in more than 2300 schools. We are dedicated to inspiring keen interest in mathematics, developing strong scholarship in the subject, and promoting the enjoyment of mathematics in high school and two-year college students.

The Florida Association of Mu Alpha Theta (FAMAT) hosts multiple regional and invite competitions to further the goals of Mu Alpha Theta.
